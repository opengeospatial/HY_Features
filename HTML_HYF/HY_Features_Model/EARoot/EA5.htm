The HY_HydrometricFeature feature type provides a concept of a monitoring station at which data on water are obtained, which may realize the logical catchment either separately, or as part of the hydrometric network (Figure 46).<br /></p><p><br /></p><p>A hydrometric feature associates a <i>position on river </i>which could be used to specify a feature of interest as location of hydrologic significance such as a catchment outlet that may realize the conceptual nexus of the corresponding catchment.<br /></p><p><br /></p><p>HY_HydrometricFeature associates the <i>hydrometricNetwork</i> in which it participates, and a <i>positionOnRiver</i>. <br /></p>
