The HY_WaterBodyStratum feature type describes a horizontal layer in a stratified waterbody determined by differences in thermal or salinity characteristics or by oxygen or nutrient content, or by virtual storage zones of a reservoir. <br /></p><p><br /></p><p>HY_WaterBodyStratum carries the properties: <i>stratumType</i> and <i>benchmark</i>.<br /></p>
