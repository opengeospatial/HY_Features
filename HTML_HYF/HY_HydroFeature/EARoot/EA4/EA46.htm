The HY_Reservoir feature type describes the water body, either natural or man-made, used for storage, regulation and control of water resources. The reservoir concept refers to a volume of water managed in zones between operating levels. <br /></p><p><br /></p><p>HY_Reservoir associates to a reservoir the <i>storedWaterBody</i> and a <i>reservoirZone</i>. <br /></p>
