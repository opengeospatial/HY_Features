The HY_Channel feature type specializes the HY_Depression class with respect to a natural or man-made, open or closed channel through or along which water may flow, or not. A channel is part of the network of channels and depressions which forms the connecting system for the hydrographic network; a channel may have vertical sections at right angles to the main (average) direction of flow or along its centerline. <br /></p><p><br /></p><p>HY_Channel associates the <i>channelNetwork</i> in which it participates. It carries the associations: <i>stream</i>, <i>bedProfileTransversal</i> and <i>bedProfileLongitudinal</i>. HY_Channel inherits from generalization the <i>confinedWaterBody</i> association and the constraints <i>outflow-at-benchmark</i>. An <i>outflow-at-profile</i> constraint emphasizes the recognition of vertical section as (said to be coincident with) the outlet of the catchment that is realized by the channel network the station is part of.<br /></p>
